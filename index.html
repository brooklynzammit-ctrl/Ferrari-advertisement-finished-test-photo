Ferrari 488 Pista

Racing Spirit, Road Perfection

TRACK-DERIVED SUPERCAR

The 488 Pista is pure racing perfection. Derived directly from Ferrari's victorious 488 GTE race car, every component has been engineered for raw speed and precision handling. With 710 horsepower from a naturally aspirated V8, it's the ultimate expression of Ferrari's racing philosophy on public roads.
